Flavorful Restaurant Dining in Lekki Lagos

Keyz restaurant brings classic intercontinental dining to Lekki Lagos in collaboration with our partner Chef Hollagold, one of Rome's most celebrated chefs who has been awarded numerous Michelin Stars over his career. Experience locally sourced, seasonal ingredients through homemade pastas, frsh seafood and traditional desserts. Complement your dinner with aged selections from the cheese cave. The restaurant experience is elevated with wine dinners, cooking classes, tastings, workshops and other culinary opportunities
